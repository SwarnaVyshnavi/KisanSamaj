Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

An initiative launched by the NDA government in 2015.Vision-To promote organic farming. Use of traditional resources.Mission- Farmers are encouraged to form clusters or groups. The aim is to form 10,000 clusters over the coming years. To bring five lakh acres of agricultural land under organic farming. Certification costs are to be borne by the government. PKVY is an elaborated component of SHM of major project NMSA.
